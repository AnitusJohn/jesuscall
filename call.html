How did a slave become a saviour?

Joseph was thrown into the pit by his brothers because they hated him. He was sold as a slave where he was wrongfully convicted and thrown into prison. The Lord was with Joseph. He lifted him up to a high level in life which no mind could comprehend even to such a high level of being placed to rule a nation. And through that Joseph saved the people of a nation and also his family from the widespread famine. You may say I am oppressed and have lost everything, but be encouraged, He will lead you to greater heights and the hand of God will be

All things are possible with Jesus

In the Bible we see a woman suffering with an issue of blood for 12 long years. No physician could bring healing to her. As she touched the hem of Jesus’s garment, His power flowed into her and immediately healed her. She rose up to a high level of having perfect health with new organs created by the power of the Lord. Moreover the Lord Jesus made her a testimony before all the crowds of people as to how she received healing through Jesus and He filled her with His peace.

God has given me the vision to teach you how to be empowered to testify about Jesus to others and bring miracles to them and not only just to receive healing or miracles for yourself.
